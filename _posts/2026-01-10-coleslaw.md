When we make [pulled pork]({% post_url 2025-12-21-pulled-pork-roast %}) sandwiches for our monthly extended family dinner, this, [potato salad]({% post_url 2021-07-16-instant-pot-potato-salad %}), and/or [baked beans]({% post_url 2022-09-25-grandmas-baked-beans %}) are usually on the side.

### Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix (shredded cabbage and carrots)
- 1/4 cup onion, minced

#### Dressing

- 2 tablespoons sugar (or sucralose)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon celery seed
- 1/4 cup whole milk
- 1/4 cup buttermilk
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1 1/2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice

### Directions

1. In a large bowl, toss to combine the coleslaw mix and minced onion. Set aside.
1. In a separate bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients until smooth.
1. Pour the dressing over the coleslaw mixture and toss to coat evenly.
1. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
1. Serve chilled.
